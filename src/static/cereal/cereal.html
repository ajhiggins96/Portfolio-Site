Cereal Nutritonal Information

Learn exactly what's in your morning cereal so you can start your day off right.

Explore the visualizations on this page to learn more!

Data source: US Department of Agriculture's National Nutrient Database for Standard Reference .

Cereal Macronutrients

There is no dearth of brands to choose from when it comes to breakfast cereals. This tool makes that decision easier, by showing the average sugar and fat content in cereals from the most popular brands. Moreover, it also allows users to filter down and select individual cereals based on total calories or a specific macronutrient

Calories & Sugar content of popular brands

Calories and Sugar are often the first nutrients one considers when deciding which ready to eat cereal to consume in the morning. Depending on energy needs, each person may prefer a different calorie/sugar tradeoff. This tool will help compare cereals and discover which may suite individual nutrition needs.

Cereal Micronutrients

This vizualization allows you to compare micronutrients of the 4 major cereal brands. At a high level you can compare which brands have higher micronutrient value. To dig deeper, you can scroll over each colored circle to get the specific cereal and the micronutrient content. On the right, you can adjust the sliders to filter the cereals with the exact micronutrient values you are looking for.

Milk Macronutrients

Everyone knows you can't have cereal without milk! Use this tool to compare and contrast different types of milk that you might want to use in your cereal. Select a milk subgroup to see specific examples of products in that group, and select multiple to compare products across groups.

Gluten-free alternatives

This visualization helps people with Gluten senstivity to choose breakfast cereals from different brands and types. You can also selects cereals based on sugar, calorie and protein intake. Use the dropdown and/or sliders to make your selections.
